How To Notify Me

It will offer three ways to let you select one which will be used to notify you if your partner choose to notify you. The three ways are: the system beep, play a piece of music or send you an email. You must choose a music file if you choose the second way and you should input your email address and it's SMTP server's name in the corresponding text fields if you prefer to receive an email. The Preplay function will let you experience the action first.
